History of the motorcycle

The first production motorcycle came out in 1894, while the famous Harley Davidson started production in 1904.
Although Harley Davidson is know as the American motorcycle company Indian Motorcycles started in 1903 right in Illinois, two years before Harley which is based in Wisconsin.
